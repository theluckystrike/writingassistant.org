❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does the synonym replacement work?
We use a built-in dictionary of 200+ word pairs to replace common words with suitable synonyms while preserving meaning.
Can I change the tone of my paragraph?
Yes, choose from formal, casual, academic, or creative tones. The engine adjusts vocabulary and sentence structure accordingly.
Is there a word limit?
The tool accepts up to 500 words per input. Word counters show exact counts for both original and rewritten versions.
How is similarity percentage calculated?
We compare word overlap and sequence similarity between original and rewritten text using a simple Jaccard-like measure.
Is my text stored or sent to any server?
No. Everything runs in your browser. No data is uploaded or stored externally.
