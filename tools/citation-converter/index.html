⚙️ Style notes

  • APA: up to 20 authors, then et al. (first 19 + … + last).
  • MLA: 3+ authors → et al. after first.
  • Chicago: up to 10 authors; 11+ → et al.
  • No author → title moves to first.
  • No date → “n.d.” (APA/Chicago) or “n.d.” (MLA).
